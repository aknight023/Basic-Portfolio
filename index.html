About

I graduated from Penn State University with a Bachelor of Sciences in Information Sciences and Technology and a Minor in Security Risk Analysis. I currently work at Liberty Mutual Insurance as an Information Security Analyst. I am currently exploring a new career path of trying to broaden my skills in coding, problem solving, and being more hands on. As Tim cook, CEO of Apple, says “it’s better to learn to code than learn English as a second language.” I would 100% have to agree with him on that. Coding is the language of the future.
